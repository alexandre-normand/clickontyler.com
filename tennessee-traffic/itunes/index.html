Description

Special $1.99 sale to celebrate the release of version 2.0!

Tennessee Traffic Advisor is the best way to stay informed about the latest Tennessee traffic conditions using your iPhone or iPod Touch. Our always up-to-date traffic data comes directly from the Tennessee Department of Transportation.

● Live traffic cams from all over the state
● Up-to-the-minute accident reports
● Roadway and construction details
● View full-color previews of interstate message signs.
● Blazingly fast data refreshing. Our new GDOT data feeds are highly compressed to make sure even the slowest cellphone connection doesn't slow down your traffic data.
● Bookmark your favorite traffic cameras for fast access.
● Share traffic camera images via email, SMS, or save them to your phone.
● Average traffic speeds along the major highways
● Supports Nashville, Knoxville, Memphis, Chattanooga, and all the interstates in between

Not only do we have the most data of any traffic app, but we're also the easiest to use. Scroll around our detailed map, or let us show the accidents and cameras closest to you - automatically.

Tennessee Traffic Advisor gives you the full power of TDOT's website when you need it the most - on the go.
