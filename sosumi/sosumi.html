Sosumi for Mac

A free Find My iPhone client for your mac

Sosumi is a simple way to find your iPhone using your Mac. Just login with your Apple ID and let Sosumi help you locate your missing device. Mac OS X 10.6 (Snow Leopard) required. Source code available on GitHub.
