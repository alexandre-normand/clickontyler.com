Nottingham

In the spirit of Notational Velocity, Nottingham is an elegant notepad for Mac OS X. Designed to be lightweight and easy to use, it's a breath of fresh air perfect for storing whatever is on your mind. Best of all, it keeps your data safe by syncing to your iPhone and the web via Simplenote.

New in Version 2.0

  • Crazy-fast syncing to your iPhone and the web using Simplenote.
  • View your notes in portrait or landscape mode.
  • Tags!
  • Store your files as plain text notes so you can easily sync with Dropbox.
  • View fully customizable Markdown previews of your notes.
  • Navigate quickly between your notes with WikiLinks.
  • Completely keyboard navigable — no mouse needed.

Compatibility & Upgrades

  • Compatible with Mac OS X 10.6.8 and above
  • 10.5 Leopard users can download version 1.0
